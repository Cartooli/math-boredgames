üìö What Is Multiplication?

Multiplication is repeated addition. Instead of adding the same number many times, we multiply! We use the multiplication sign (√ó) or sometimes a dot (‚Ä¢).

üéØ Key Takeaways

  • Use the √ó sign to show multiplication
