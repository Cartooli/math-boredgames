üìö What Are Patterns?

A pattern is something that repeats following a rule. A sequence is a list of numbers or objects in a specific order.

Number Pattern: 5, 10, 15, 20... (add 5 each time)

üîç Finding the Rule

Steps to Find the Rule:
1. Look at the difference between numbers
2. Is it adding? Subtracting? Multiplying?
3. Test your rule on the next number
4. Use the rule to predict what comes next
