üìö What is a Percent?

Percent means "per 100" or "out of 100." The symbol is %.

Examples:
50% = 50 out of 100 = 50/100 = 1/2 = 0.5
25% = 25 out of 100 = 25/100 = 1/4 = 0.25
100% = the whole thing
0% = nothing

üîÑ Conversions

Percent ‚Üí Decimal: Divide by 100 (move decimal 2 left)
75% = 0.75

Decimal ‚Üí Percent: Multiply by 100 (move decimal 2 right)
0.3 = 30%

Percent ‚Üí Fraction: Put over 100, simplify
40% = 40/100 = 2/5

üßÆ Finding Percentages

To find a percent of a number: convert to decimal, then multiply!

Example: What is 20% of 50?
20% = 0.20
0.20 √ó 50 = 10

üí° Example

Example: Sale Price

Problem: A $40 shirt is 25% off. What's the discount?

Solution: 25% of 40 = 0.25 √ó 40 = $10 off (sale price = $30)
