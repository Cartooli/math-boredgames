üìê Perimeter: Distance Around

Perimeter is the distance around the outside of a shape. Add up all the sides!

Example: A rectangle with sides 5 cm and 3 cm
Perimeter = 5 + 3 + 5 + 3 = 16 cm

üì¶ Area: Space Inside

Area is the space inside a shape. We measure it in square units.

Rectangle Area Formula: length √ó width
Example: 5 cm √ó 3 cm = 15 square cm

Example 2: Area of a Rectangle

Problem: A garden is 6 feet long and 4 feet wide. What's the area?
