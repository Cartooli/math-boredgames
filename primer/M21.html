üìö What Is an Equation?

An equation is a mathematical statement that two expressions are equal. Our goal: find the value of the variable!

Example: x + 5 = 12
What number plus 5 equals 12? ‚Üí x = 7

‚öñÔ∏è The Balance Method

Whatever you do to one side, do to the other to keep it balanced!

Example: x + 3 = 10
Subtract 3 from both sides:
x + 3 ‚àí 3 = 10 ‚àí 3
x = 7 ‚úì

üéØ Two-Step Equations

Step 2: Divide both sides by 2 ‚Üí x = 4

Example: Two-Step

üéØ Key Takeaways
