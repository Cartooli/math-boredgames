What is Properties of Multiplication?

3rd Grade Formula Available

Understanding multiplication properties. The key formula is Commutative, Associative, Distributive. This concept is typically introduced in 3rd Grade and involves multiplication. Multiplication is repeated addition, used to find the total of equal groups.

Key Formula

Commutative, Associative, Distributive
